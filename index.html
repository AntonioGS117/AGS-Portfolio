About Me

Welcome to my portfolio page. Feel free to send any feedback via email located in my Contact Information . This is one of my first website I have created and will continue to create more responsive, interactive and engaging websites going forward. Having some experience prior to taking this coding class has help me understand the overall idea of Web-building. I hope to completely immerse myself in writing code and continue to develope ideas for websites.

I became interested in Web Building two years ago. However, looking from outside in, I was intimidated by the lengthy process of learning the different coding languages in the ecosystem. After taking the courses provided by Austin Coding Academy, learning to code has felt more feasible to grasp. I appreciate the constant aid, support and guidance the instructors have given me.
